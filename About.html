About Location

Welcome to Guptakashi, a quaint town nestled in the beautiful state of Uttarakhand, India. Guptakashi is located on the banks of the River Mandakini and is known for its spiritual and cultural significance. Guptakashi is named after the legendary sage, Maharishi Gupta, who meditated here for many years. The town is also believed to be the place where Lord Shiva hid from the Pandavas during the Mahabharata period. The ancient temples of Kedarnath and Badrinath are located near Guptakashi, making it an important stop for pilgrims. Apart from its religious significance, Guptakashi is also a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. The town is surrounded by stunning Himalayan peaks and offers a range of outdoor activities such as trekking, camping, and white-water rafting. In Guptakashi, you can experience the best of both worlds - the tranquility and spirituality of a small town, as well as the thrill and adventure of the great outdoors. And at the end of a long day of exploration and adventure, you can relax and rejuvenate in one of the town's many guesthouses or hotels. At Guptakashi, you'll find a warm and welcoming community, as well as stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Come and discover all that this magical town has to offer!
